"Mad Max" is a cult classic Australian post-apocalyptic action film series created by George Miller. The series began with the release of "Mad Max" in 1979, starring Mel Gibson as the titular character, Max Rockatansky. The film is set in a dystopian future where society has collapsed, and law and order have disintegrated, leaving only roving gangs and marauders.

Mission impossible

"Mission: Impossible" is a popular action spy film franchise that originated from the 1966 television series of the same name. The film series kicked off in 1996 with the release of "Mission: Impossible," directed by Brian De Palma and starring Tom Cruise as the protagonist, Ethan Hunt. Cruise has since become synonymous with the franchise, reprising his role in subsequent installments.
