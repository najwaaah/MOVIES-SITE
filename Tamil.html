96

"96" is a Tamil-language romantic drama film released in 2018. Directed by C. Prem Kumar, the film stars Vijay Sethupathi and Trisha Krishnan in the lead roles. The story revolves around two high school sweethearts, Ram and Janu, who meet again after 22 years at a school reunion and reminisce about their past. The film beautifully captures the nostalgia, emotions, and complexities of their relationship.

Diesel

"Diesel" is a 2023 action thriller film directed by David Twohy and starring Vin Diesel. The movie follows the story of a retired Navy SEAL who is drawn back into the world of espionage when his mentor is kidnapped by a terrorist organization. As he races against time to rescue his mentor and stop the terrorists' plot, he must confront his own demons and utilize all of his skills to survive.
